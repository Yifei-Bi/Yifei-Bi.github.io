Scientific research experience
==============================
During my undergraduate studies, I primarily worked under the supervision of Professor Yong Yue and Professor Xiaohui Zhu on various research projects. The first project focused on NLP automatic scoring, with the objective of deploying the scoring system on the Moodle website. My main responsibility was the development of the Moodle website. Unfortunately, due to various reasons, the project concluded prematurely, and as a result, it was not included in my resume. 

The second project revolved around cyanobacteria detection, which was a computer vision project inherited from previous cohorts. Its main objective was to identify and segment cyanobacteria on the surfaces of lakes such as Jinji Lake and Dushu Lake in Suzhou. The purpose of this project was to aid relevant departments in environmental maintenance. 

In the third project, we focused on object detection, specifically targeting mask detection in kitchen environments. One of the significant challenges in this project was the diverse angles of the dataset, as the images were captured by cameras. In this area, we enhanced and improved the dataset, leading to the successful publication of a paper at the ICCCR conference.

Finally, as a personal research endeavor, I embarked on replicating and learning from the AlphaStar model. I designed a simple AI system based on reinforcement learning. 

These experiences encapsulate my research background.
